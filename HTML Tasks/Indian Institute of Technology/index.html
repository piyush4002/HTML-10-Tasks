Indian Institute of Technology

The Indian Institute of Technology (IITs) are autonomous public institutes of higher education, located in India. They are governed by the Indian Institute of Technology Act, 1961 which has declared them as institutes of national importance alongside National institutes og Technology and lays down their powers, duties, and framework for governance etc.
